What is "Co-op Unit".

Co-op Unit or "Gaming Tinder" is a mobile app dedicated to gamers. It works exactly like the well known app "Tinder".

With this app, you can select what kind of game you would like to play, then you select ( depending on the game ) the role of your partner ( tank, carry, supp, etc ) and also his level.
